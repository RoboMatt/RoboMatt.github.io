Some of the Projects I've Done.
I am not the greatest developer, but I learn through projects. These are the public projects that I have worked on, I hope you enjoy.

Griswold Drama

This is the home page for my school's drama site. This site involves ticket purchasing, form registration, media and photo sharing, and much more. This project was fun for many reasons. One is that I learned a lot, and this was my first time doing better with web styling. The biggest thing, however, was that this was essentially my first commission. I was told by my director to do this project, and given an end goal and deadlines. This would be my first public project, which was seen over 3000 people!

Griswold Drama Extras

This page is my pride and joy. This is the playground of the whole drama site, the place I put all the fun or unimportant projects and tests I work on. Here lives games such as Pong and Snake, that my whole school uses, as well as useful applications such as the Motivational Clock or Pitch Pipe. These are all ideas that I had, that I decided to work out on my own. It was good fun working on something to try and get it just how I imagined, and that is what the Extras site represents.
